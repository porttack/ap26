# John Gruber — The Man Who Fixed HTML

## Who Is He?

John Gruber is a writer and developer best known for creating
**Markdown** in 2004. He runs [Daring Fireball](https://daringfireball.net).

![John Gruber](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/6/64/John_Gruber%2C_2009_%28cropped%29.jpg)

## What Did He Build?

- **Markdown** — plain text that converts to HTML automatically
- **Daring Fireball** — his blog, running since 2002
